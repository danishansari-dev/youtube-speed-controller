# Privacy Policy for Universal Video Speed Controller

**Effective Date:** May 13, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Universal Video Speed Controller extension ("the Extension") collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Universal Video Speed Controller is designed with privacy in mind. **The Extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on remote servers.** All processing and data storage occurs strictly locally on your device.

### Permissions Used:
To function correctly, the Extension requests the following browser permissions:
- **`storage`**: Used to save your settings, preferences (e.g., custom playback speeds, theme, shortcut configurations), and local analytics (such as time saved) directly on your device.
- **`activeTab`** & **`tabs`**: Used exclusively to detect video players on the active webpage you are viewing so the Extension can overlay its controls and apply your requested playback speeds.

### Local Analytics
The Extension includes an "Insights" panel that calculates statistics like "Time saved" and your "Most used" speeds. **This data is generated and stored locally on your hard drive.** It is never uploaded, tracked, or shared with us or any third parties.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party tracking services, analytics frameworks (such as Google Analytics), or advertising SDKs. Your browsing history and habits are never monitored or monetized.

## 3. Data Security

Because all data remains on your local machine, the security of your settings and local analytics is tied directly to the security of your device and browser. 

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time if we introduce new features that require different permissions. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy or the Extension's behavior, please feel free to open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/danishansari-dev/youtube-speed-controller).
